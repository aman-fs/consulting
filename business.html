Business Immigration

Learn how to successfully prepare for the student visa interview.

Our role will be to help you set up a business in Canada and secure residency for Key persons. Here's a guide to help you navigate this process, along with potential benefits:

1. Understand Why we need to go for Business Immigration and set up a base in Canada
  • Understand the benefits of Business Immigration. Why do you need to expand your company base and set up abroad especially when you are doing very well in your home country?
  • Understand the opportunities and set up strategy – In one-on-one sessions with our representative.
    • 2. Business Structure and Registration:
  • Identify the province and city where you want to operate.
  • Choose a suitable business structure (e.g., corporation, partnership) and register your business with the appropriate authorities in the province or territory where you plan to operate.
    • 3. Business Plan:
  • Develop a comprehensive business plan outlining your company's goals, market analysis, financial projections, and details about key personnel.
    • 4. Legal Consultation:
  • Seek legal advice to understand the legal requirements, regulations, and implications of setting up a business in Canada
    • 5. Residency Options:
  • Process the Closed Visa for key persons
  • Explore residency options for key persons, such as the Express Entry system, Provincial Nominee Programs (PNPs), etc
    • 6. Obtain a Business Number (BN):
  • Register for a Business Number (BN) with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) for tax purposes.
    • 7. Open a Business Bank Account:
  • Open a business bank account in Canada to facilitate financial transactions related to your business operations.
    • 8. Employment and Labour Laws:
  • Understand Canadian employment and labor laws, including regulations related to hiring and employing foreign workers.
    • 9. Taxation:
  • Familiarize yourself with Canadian tax laws and obligations for businesses. We will connect you with the best in the Industry
    • 10. Benefits of Setting Up in Canada:
  • Economy: Canada has a stable and robust economy with access to global markets.
  • Skilled Workforce: Benefit from a well-educated and skilled workforce.
  • Quality of Life: Canada offers a high quality of life, making it an attractive destination for businesses and employees.
  • Innovation and Technology: Access to advanced technology and innovation hubs.
  • Trade Agreements: Canada has trade agreements with various countries, providing business opportunities.
  • Business Risk Mitigation: This will be discussed by our representative in one one-on-one discussion with your key persons
    • 11. Networking:
  • Connect with local business networks, industry associations, and chambers of commerce to build relationships and stay informed about the business environment.
    • 12. Compliance:
  • Ensure compliance with all business and immigration regulations to avoid legal issues.
    • 13. Continuous Monitoring:
  • Stay informed about changes in regulations, tax laws, and immigration policies that may impact your business. We will be your mentors and guide in this.
    • 14. Human Resources and Employee Benefits:
  • Develop a competitive employee benefits package to attract and retain key personnel. We will help you understand the federal and provincial government support with can be the key differentiator in your decision to set up in Canada.
